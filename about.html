Who We Are

Together We Can Heal The Earth

EcoWatch is a grassroots movement dedicated to monitoring pollution, restoring forests, and empowering communities across Kenya and East Africa.

Founded in 2024 by Joseph Ocnieng, EcoWatch began with a simple observation: our environment is changing, and we need data to understand how. What started as a school project has grown into a comprehensive platform tracking air quality, water purity, and industrial emissions.

We believe that technology and nature can coexist. By using low-cost sensors and community reporting, we are making environmental data accessible to everyone—from policymakers to local farmers.
